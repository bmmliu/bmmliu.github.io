üëã I am a Ph.D. student in the **Civil and Environmental Engineering Department** at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where I focus on cutting-edge research in **intelligent transportation systems** and **autonomous driving**. I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from New York University in 2024 and my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of California, Davis.

My research interests span **large language models**, **reinforcement learning**, and **deep learning**, with particular emphasis on applications to **smart mobility systems**, **autonomous driving**, and **intersection safety**. I am passionate about developing AI-driven solutions that can make transportation safer and more efficient.
